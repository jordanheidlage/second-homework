About Me

Jordan Heidlage is a 29 year old Operations Manager based out of Cincinnati, Ohio. Jordan graduated from Miami University with his B.A. in Biology, and a minor in Anthropology in 2017. Shortly upon graduation, Jordan completed a 12 week Management Training Program through the Kohl's corporation, and was directly placed as the Operations Manager in their highest volume store in the nation in Dublin, Ohio. Several years later, Jordan joined Amazon.com inc. Where he excelled as an operations leader, and was given the task of directing the operations of Amazon's Luxury Stores Program Vogue Article On The Project. After that, Jordan went on to launch multiple Sub Same Day sites throughout the region, leading teams of +250 people to launch same day delivery service for Amazon Customers. Jordan Currently resides in Cincinnati, with his girlfriend, and Golden Retriever, Kevin.
